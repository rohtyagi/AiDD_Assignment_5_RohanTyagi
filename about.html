About Me

I'm a Master of Science in Information Systems student at Indiana University, with a B.S. in Information Technology (Highest Distinction). I enjoy applying analytics, automation, and human‑centered design to streamline workflows and support better decisions.

Recently, I built Power Apps to digitize inspections and operations, delivered Power BI dashboards for executive forecasting, and created reusable design systems to scale consistent, branded solutions. I also support students as a Graduate Assistant for Business Analytics and Modeling, focusing on descriptive, predictive, and prescriptive techniques.

Outside of class and work, I’m relearning piano, volunteering as an instructor, and staying curious about product design and technology ethics.
